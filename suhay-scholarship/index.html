GIVING

HELPING STUDENTS ACHIEVE THEIR DREAMS

Suhay Scholarship is one of the pillars of the Orani Suhay Foundation in alleviating poverty in the towns of Orani, Samal, and Hermosa, and adjacent towns in Bataan.

Suhay Scholarship

Education as the main driver of enlightening minds and improving lives, the Suhay Scholarship provides grants to students in their final year in college and with little means to complete their education. Also included in the program are students who wish to study for their board examinations review. Initially, the scholarship took under its wings engineering graduating students and later expanded to cover other courses such as architecture, accountancy, IT, psychology, and others. To date, more than 100 students have benefitted from the program and have been directly employed at DMCI’s various companies.

Our Mission

Why we support
Scholars?

Nelson Mandela once said: “Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.”

An educated person has better chances of finding a good employment and will be able to help his or her own family to have a better life. Suhay Scholarship helps qualified students or those who are reviewing for their board examinations needing financial assistance. Any indigent student from Orani, Samal or Hermosa who are in their final years in college or are planning to enroll at board examination review courses are encouraged to apply for scholarship.

APPLY FOR SCHOLARSHIP

How to Apply for Scholarships

Transcript of Records

Submit your latest transcript of records for the previous school years (1st to 3rd years).

Letter of Intent

Provide a letter of intent by your parents, guardian or sponsor explaining why the need for the scholarship

Additional Requirements

For identification, provide your Baranggay clearance, Cedula, and Valid IDs

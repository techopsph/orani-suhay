David M. Consunji believed that the people of Orani need just one opportunity to rise up against life’s hardships and be able to pass on good deeds to the community. With his desire to give back, the Orani Suhay Foundation was born. It is committed in empowering the people of Orani and neighboring towns to help improve their lives through strategic scholarships and livelihood programs. More

About Orani Suhay

HELPING EDUCATION

Suhay Scholarship continues to provide the necessary aid for graduating college students to complete their education. To date, over 100 Suhay Scholars proudly earned their college diplomas and board certifications. They have now made a difference in their families’ lives being gainfully employed at DMCI.

SOCIAL ENTREPRENEURSHIP

SSUWAK provides livelihood programs for the less fortunate people of Orani and neighboring towns. Artisans and talented handicraft volunteers create stellar decor pieces using Capiz shells as main material. SSUWAK’s creations adorn many abodes and have exhibited in multiple trade fairs.

SCHOLARSHIP

Improving Lives Through Education

Orani Suhay Foundation, through Suhay Scholarship, seeks to help the less fortunate students of Orani to finish their college studies. A bona fide graduating college student from Orani in the fields of architecture, accountancy, engineering, or psychology courses with good scholastic standing are able to apply for funding. Suhay Scholarship has helped over a hundred indigent students fulfill their dreams of completing college education and help their families suffering from poverty.
